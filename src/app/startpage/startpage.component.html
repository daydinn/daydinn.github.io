Self-Optimization Demo Applikation

Empower your mental, physical, and emotional aspects. Unlock a better you today.


This demo application was developed by Diyar Aydin as part of his bachelor's project entitled "Development and Analysis of a Support App for Self-Optimization". It has been designed to answer the question of how a self-help application should be designed to help users achieve their personal goals and improve their quality of life. The focus of the project is on 3 aspects of self-optimisation, namely mental, physical and productive, represented by green red and blue colours.


Users are able to:

  • Log their activities, and mods
  • Create, complete and delete activities and todos
  • Track their activities & modes in detail, add notes, and compare with dates
  • Practice guided breathing and physical exercises
  • Get advice from the app based on their mood
  • Earn points and level up in different aspects of self-optimisation


The main points that distinguish this application from others are that it offers the user a completely personal and free experience. It allows users to prioritise their own preferences and create their own way of self-optimisation, which combined with its self-monitoring features can encourage users to be more self-aware and self-reflective. And it integrates different areas of self-optimisation with gamification elements on a single platform which can be used on both desktop and mobile. The design has been kept intimate and user-friendly to focus on the main use.

However, it should be kept in mind that this is not a full application, but rather a prototype implementation to better understand user preferences and the effectiveness of such an application. There may be some performance and loading issues.
